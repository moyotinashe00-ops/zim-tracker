# Volt: Atlas Intelligence Walkthrough

The **Volt Atlas** has been upgraded from a static visualization tool to a live intelligence dashboard. It now leverages AI and real-time community telemetry to provide a comprehensive view of the national grid.

## New Intelligence Features

### 1. AI Strategic Summary
- **National Perspective**: A new overlay card at the top of the map provides a 1-sentence strategic summary generated by Gemini.
- **Dynamic Analysis**: The AI analyzes all currently offline suburbs and identifies clusters or patterns in the load-shedding distribution.

### 2. Pulsing Outage Markers
- **Active Alerts**: Suburbs currently without power now feature a pulsing Neon Red glow.
- **Visual Priority**: This animation draws immediate attention to areas in crisis, allowing users to distinguish between "Active" and "Offline" nodes at a glance.

### 3. Live Report Ticker
- **Community Telemetry**: A scrolling marquee at the bottom of the map displays the 10 most recent community reports from the last 24 hours.
- **Verified Pulse**: Provides users with a "boots on the ground" perspective of the grid's health.

### 4. Advanced Zone Inspector
- **Confidence Scoring**: Tapping a zone now reveals a "Confidence Indicator"—a 5-bar scale showing how reliable the current status data is based on report frequency.
- **Status Breakdown**: Clear separation between official status and community reports.

## Technical Details
- **Gemini Integration**: Uses the new `getMapIntelligenceSummary` method in `AIService`.
- **Marquee Engine**: Custom horizontal ticker built for 60fps scrolling on the map view.
- **Telemetry Stream**: Leverages a optimized `recentReportsStream` in `HomeViewModel` to minimize Firestore reads while maintaining real-time accuracy.
